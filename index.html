Murdle
How does it work?

It is just Wordle with friends. It goes like this..

You invite a friend to play with you.

The friend accepts your invitation.

You give your friend a difficult word to solve.

Your friend gives you an even more difficult word to solve.

The game begins.

You both attempt your respective words and hopefully one of you solves it and is the winner.

If both of you solve your words then the one who solves it in more tries is the winner.

Is that so?

I was kidding. The one who solves it in less number of tries is the winner.

Obviously the loser of the first game does not give up that easily.

So you have a rematch.

The loser of the second game also does not give up easily so you have another game.

This game goes on and on till you play so many games that the game database has no space left for storing the game results or till you run out of your limited knowledge of 5 letter words or if many millions start playing this game to bring down this web site and the internet or if the sun explodes and becomes a quasar (is that even possible? Dont ask me I am just a programmer). If you have had enough of this ridiculous background just click on the login button. This will have two options - Log In and Sign Up. You must Sign Up with the web site before proceeding to the Log In step. Note we will not ever sell your email to anyone. We are not in that business. Usually Log In will be required only once in the sense that once you have logged in successfully, the device from where you are logging in will save your log in particulars on the device and will not force you to log in again when you again access the web site.

Well you ask 'I have already Signed Up and and logged in but I cannot find my friend xyz in the 'Other Murdlers' section?'

That is a very good question.

Get your friend to also Sign Up on the web site and once he does that you will surely see him in the 'Other Murdlers' section.

By the way you will continue to see him in the 'Other Murdlers' section even if you have lost to him so many times that you do not want to see him any more!! Etiquette dictates you just ignore him and find a weaker Murdler you can beat!!

Seriously speaking the game goes like this..

  1. You click on the login button and select Sign Up to register on the web site
  2. Now log in if needed.
  3. You should see other players in the Other Murdlers section.
  4. Click on the players to select who you want to play with.
  5. Now select a 5 letter word for your opponent in the 'Word for' section and click on the Invite button.
  6. Your opponent will immediately see your invitation pop up in the 'Invitations to play' section on his screen.
  7. Your opponent will select you from amongst the invitations he/she has received and select a 5 letter word for you and click on Accept Invite button.
  8. After this you and your opponent should both see a game listed in 'Current Games' section.
  9. Click on the game and start playing.
  10. The result will appear in the 'Completed Games' section as soon as the match is over.
  11. Note that for solving game you will use the on-screen keyboard but for sending your word with the invitation and accepting the invitation with your own word you will be using the device keyboard.

Murdle
Background

It is just Wordle with friends. It goes like this..

You invite a friend over to play with you.

The friend accepts your invitation.

You give your friend a word to solve.

Your friend gives you even more difficult word to solve.

The game begins.

You both attempt your respective words and hopefully one of you solves it and is the winner.

If both of you solve your words then the one who solves it in more tries is the winner.

Is that so?

I was kidding. The one who solves it in less number of tries is the winner.

Obviously the loser of the first game does not give up that easily.

So you have a rematch.

The loser of the second game also does not give up easily so you have another game.

This game goes on and on till the you play so many games that the game database has no space left for storing the game results or till you run out of your limited knowledge of 5 letter words or if so many millions start playing this game to bring down this web site and the internet or if the sun explodes and becomes a quasar (is that even possible? Dont ask me I am just a programmer). If you have had enough with this ridiculous background just click on the register button to register yourself and if you have already registered then just log in and start playing.

Well you ask 'I have already registered and logged in but I cannot find my friend?'

That is a very good question.

Get your friend to also register and log in and then you will see him in the friends section.

By the way you will continue to see him in the friends section even if you have lost to him so many times that you do not consider him your friend any more!! Etiquette dictates you just ignore him and find a weaker friend you can beat!!
